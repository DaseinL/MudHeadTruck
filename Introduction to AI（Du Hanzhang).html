Introduction to AI

Artificial Intelligence, usually abbreviated as AI. It is a new technological science that studies and develops theories, methods, techniques and application systems used to simulate, extend and expand human intelligence.

Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science. It attempts to understand the essence of intelligence and produce a new intelligent machine that can respond in a similar way to human intelligence. Research in this field includes robotics, language recognition, image recognition, Natural language processing and expert systems, etc.

Artificial intelligence is the study of making computers to simulate certain human thinking processes and intelligent behaviors such as learning, reasoning, thinking, planning, etc.. It mainly includes the principles of computer realization of intelligence, manufacturing computers similar to human brain intelligence, and making computers Can achieve higher level applications. This process also involves other issues such as consciousness, self, mind, unconscious mind and so on. In other words, artificial intelligence includes disciplines such as computer science, psychology, philosophy, and linguistics. It can be said that almost all disciplines of natural sciences and social sciences, whose content has far exceeded the scope of computer science.
